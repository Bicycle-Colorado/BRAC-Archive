2011 Road Awards Party - Top 3 BAR BAT - September 17, 2011

Home / 2011 Road Awards Party - Top 3 BAR BAT - September 17, 2011
Submitted by brac on Tue, 2011-08-02 11:31

For 2011, the ACA will host two separate awards parties. 

On Saturday, September 17, 2011, from 6-9 pm, we will recognize the achievements from our 2011 Road Race Season.

On Saturday, February 4, 2012, from 6-9 pm, we will recognize the achievements from our 2011 Cross Race Season.  

Both events will be held at the American Mountaineering Center in Golden.  The events will include Basil Doc's pizza, Great Divide and mix1 refreshments, snacks, and soda, and an awards ceremony in the Center's Foss Theater.  We will have plenty of raffles and tons of fun, so please stay tuned for flyers and event details.

Awards given out:

Best All-around Rider (BAR) September Party

Best All-around Team (BAT) September Party

Cross Cup Rider (February Party)

Cross Cup Team (February Party)

Other Recognition for Racers, Staff, Officials and Members of the ACA Community

 

Raffles include product from Rudy Project, Pactimo, mix1, Retul, and other great ACA partners.
