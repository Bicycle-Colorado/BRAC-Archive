The Legends: Phil Liggett and Paul Sherwen at Wheat Ridge Cyclery to Support Bicycles for Humanity - Saturday August 27 7pm-9pm

Home / The Legends: Phil Liggett and Paul Sherwen at Wheat Ridge Cyclery to Support Bicycles for Humanity - Saturday August 27 7pm-9pm
Submitted by brac on Mon, 2011-08-08 13:52

 


We want your old bikes!

Wheat Ridge Cyclery and Bicycles For Humanity Colorado present:

An evening of bike legends and race insights with Paul Sherwen and Phil Liggett

 All proceeds to benefit Bicycles for Humanity Colorado

 For the past two years, WRC and Bicycles for Humanity Colorado have partnered to bring donated bikes to Africa to solve basic transportation and medical access needs.

 During the inaugural USA Pro Cycling Challenge, Paul Sherwen and Phil Liggett, famed cycling commentators, have offered to help Bicycles for Humanity raise awareness about how bikes can make a big impact in Africa.

Join us Saturday, August 27, 2011 from 7:00 p.m. - 9:00 p.m. at WRC for a fantastic evening of bike racing stories from around the globe, including this year?s Tour de France.

 Bring a bike to donate (must be in working order) or $25 per person at the door

RSVP HERE or simply bring in a donation bike
and pick up a ticket starting Saturday August 20!
