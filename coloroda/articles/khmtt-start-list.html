KHMTT - Number Pick Up Events, Updated Start List & Race Communication

Home / KHMTT - Number Pick Up Events, Updated Start List & Race Communication
Submitted by brac on Sat, 2011-03-12 11:04

Since 1991, the time trials at Cherry Creek State Park have provided a great setting for Colorado racers of all ages.  Since the inclusion of the Karen Hornbostel Memorial designation, the Series has also served as a fundraiser for the Cancer Fitness Institute, an organization that helps those with cancer to maintain a healthy lifestyle and better fight the disease.

Please click here for the updated start list. 


Please note:
All racers must have a transponder to be scored at the Series!  
Rental transponders will NOT be available on race days.


 
Please click the link on the ACA website for 'transponders' to purchase your annual transponder.


This year, we will provide three number pick-up events in the weeks prior to the Series.  You must have a signed standard release form to get your number (forms available at number pick up, or print one from the ACA site under 'forms').  Because we need a signed form, you cannot pick up for a friend unless you bring their signed release form.  To avoid confusion and undue chaos on race day, please plan on getting your number at one of the following pick-up events:

Important Race Notes (This is official race communication):
 
-Numbers on Right side

-You must have a transponder, and it must be mounted correctly on your bicycle!  Please watch the ACA site for guidelines on transponder placement and additional information.

-You must pay to drive a car into the State Park.  Annual Colorado State Parks Passes can be purchased here:
 (http://parks.state.co.us/ParksPasses/Pages/ParksPassInfo.aspx) .

-Directions for parking:  Enter the park through the East Entrance on Parker Road.  Go straight through the four way stop sign and down the hill. As the road turns right, the Smoky Hill area is on the left. This is the only parking area for the event ? No Parking in the Boat Ramp area.  Please be courteous and respectful of our parking volunteer marshals and non-race traffic.

- Absolutely no warming up in the Boat Ramp area!  This email serves as your official warning.

-The Start is just south of the Smoky Hill bathrooms via the Bike path. It can also be reached on the roadway by following the signs for the Boat Launch and East Shades areas.

- Do NOT cross the finish line except when finishing your race!  If you do, your time will not be accurately recorded.  To ensure proper timing, when finishing please ride to the right side of the finish line (away from the centerline)  This posting serves as your official notification of changes made to the 2011 Series for the successful implementation of the timing system.

- Maps, updates and more are available at www.coloradocycling.org/special-events/khmtt

- PreRace has posted the start list for KHMTT.
Please doublecheck your time spot! http://www.precorsa.com/prerace/assets/KHMTT.pdf
As of Friday afternoon (March 11, 2011) PreRace has sent confirmation emails to all participants. If you have not received a confirmation email, please call them at 888-557-4020.


-2011 Series information can be found on the race flyer, and if you have any more questions, please contact the ACA?s office: 303-458-5538. 
Once again, I really appreciate your patience and understanding during the difficult registration process.  The ACA really is a tremendous community of cyclists - thank you!
