Officials Forms and Documents

Home / Officials Forms and Documents

USA Cycling trains and licenses race officials for all races that are supported and promoted by Colorado Cycling and our member clubs. Colorado Cycling coordinates with USAC to provide trainings, seminars and other events for race officials.  Please see this link to USAC for info on becoming an official.
